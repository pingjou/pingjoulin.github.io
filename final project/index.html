Homemade Cuisine and Related Stories

  • Scrambled Eggs with Tomatoes and Oyakodon
    • Scrambled Eggs with Tomatoes is a beloved and traditional Taiwanese dish that consists of stir-frying eggs and fresh tomatoes together with garlic, scallions, and soy sauce. It's a colorful and flavorful meal that is often served as a quick and comforting home-cooked dish for any time of day. Also, it is really easy to make. Therefore, I really often to make this for myself. Moreover, The Oyakodon is also easy and tasty and it is a famous cuisine in Japan. It has many different way to make so I create one of recipes in other page.
  • Three Cups Chicken
    • Three Cups Chicken is a popular and traditional Taiwanese dish that originated from southern China and has been adapted by Taiwanese chefs to create a unique version. The dish gets its name from the equal proportions of soy sauce, sesame oil, and rice wine that create a flavorful sauce that the chicken is cooked in. With ginger, garlic, and basil, Three Cups Chicken has a rich and complex flavor that makes it a favorite for family gatherings and special occasions.
